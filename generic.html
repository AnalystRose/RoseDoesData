Data Analyst / Data Strategist / Data Educator

Welcome to my data analytics portfolio.

What I love most about data is its ability to invoke thoughts, answer questions, and solve problems that ordinarily would be uphill tasks. I love that data allows us to prescribe solutions, diagnose problems, and predict future possibilities.

My goal is to use my data analytics skills to solve humanitarian problems and make the world a better, more accepting, and equitable place for all. This vision has particularly lead me to specific interest in solving problems around healthcare, food scarcity, sanitation, green energy, and other spheres currently affecting humanity.

Beyond my day-to-day work, I also have a personal project dubbed ROSEDOESDATA which is a TikTok and Youtube page where I document lessons, thoughts, and trends in, and around the data world. This passion project is dedicated to people starting out in, or rekindling there interest in data.
